If it is false that lhs is near rhs according to threshold thr, print formatted error message and then panic.

lhs and rhs are near, if relativeCloseness(lhs, rhs) < thr.

The threshold thr shall be in [0.0, 1.0].

A threshold ~0.0 implies that lhs is near rhs, and ~1.0 implies the lhs is NOT near rhs.
